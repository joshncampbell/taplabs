How it works

Simple by design.

Coast Clear is built to help people review nearby activity quickly, contribute useful reports, and make better-informed decisions before entering the water.

How to use Coast Clear
  1. Open the map to review recent shark sightings in the area.
  2. Tap a location on the map or use the add button to start a new report.
  3. Add species, time, notes, and a photo when available.
  4. Submit the sighting so others can see it on the map and in the sightings list.
Important note

Coast Clear is a community-awareness tool. Reports are user-submitted and not independently verified. Local signage, lifeguards, and official safety guidance should always take priority.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for launch, usage, and expectations.

Is Coast Clear an official safety service?

No. Coast Clear is a community-driven awareness tool. It can help people share sightings, but it does not replace official beach conditions or local safety direction.

Can users upload photos with sightings?

Yes. Reports can include optional photos, notes, species selection, and location details to provide more context.

How much does Coast Clear cost?

Nothing. Coast Clear is free to download and free to use.

Does Coast Clear track me or my information?

No. Coast Clear does not track or gather personal information. When a sighting is submitted, the app stores the report details provided and limited device information needed for the experience.
